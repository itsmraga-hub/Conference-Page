Mialoni Back To Basics Conference, Kenya 2022.

The Back to Basics conference brings together students from all over the country for a weekend of fun and reminding ourselves of the basics. It is a yearly conference and we are always looking for ways to grow our community. This year, we will be welcoming to new universities to our community.
